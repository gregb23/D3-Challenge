Age and Obesity Rates by States

For my D3 journal, I decided to show the average obesity rates by age in each state. The three states with the lowest obesity rates are Colorado, DC and Hawaii. Mississippi, Arkansas and West Virginia have the highest obesity rates.

The states with the hihgest obesity rates also have high poverty rates, higher health care cost and more smokers. An assumption can be made that all that those factors could contribulte to the high obesity rates. I did not test that assumption.

The opposite can be said for states with lower obesity rates. They tend to have lower poverty rates, lower health care costr and less smokers on average. Those factors contribute to the quality of life.
